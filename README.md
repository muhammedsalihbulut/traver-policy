# Privacy Policy for Traver

Last Updated: March 22, 2026

Traver built the Traver app as a Free app. This SERVICE is provided by Traver at no cost and is intended for use as is.

### 1. Information Collection and Use
We use Apple and Google Authentication for a secure experience. We only collect:
- Your email address (for authentication)
- Your name and bio (to display on your profile)
- The travel discoveries (photos, locations, comments) you choose to share.

### 2. Location Data
Our app allows you to share your visited locations. Location data is only collected when you explicitly create a post and have granted permission.

### 3. Data Deletion
We respect your right to privacy. You can permanently delete your entire account and all associated data directly from the "Settings" menu within the app.

### 4. Third-Party Services
We use Supabase for database management and storage. Your data is stored securely on their cloud infrastructure.
